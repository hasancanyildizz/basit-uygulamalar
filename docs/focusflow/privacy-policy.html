Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to FocusFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Pomodoro timer application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

FocusFlow stores all data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

  • Timer Settings: Focus duration, break durations, auto-start preferences
  • Task Information: Task names, estimated pomodoros, completion status
  • Statistics: Completed pomodoros, focus time, daily counts
  • Preferences: Sound settings, notification preferences

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT have access to your data

How Your Data is Used

Your data is used solely to:

  • Track your Pomodoro sessions and breaks
  • Manage your task list and progress
  • Display productivity statistics
  • Send local notifications when timers complete
  • Remember your preferred settings

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally using secure device storage
  • Data never leaves your device
  • No internet connection is required to use the app

Third-Party Services

FocusFlow does NOT integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

Audio

Sound effects are generated locally on your device. No audio data is transmitted externally.

Push Notifications

We use local push notifications to alert you when timers complete. These are generated and delivered locally on your device.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modification: Edit tasks and settings at any time
  • Deletion: Delete tasks or reset statistics

Summary

Your privacy is guaranteed because:

  • All data stays on your device
  • No servers, no data collection
  • Works completely offline
  • You have full control over your data
