Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Habit Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Habit Tracker stores all data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Habit Information: Names, colors, icons, and settings for habits you create
  • Check-in Records: Dates and times when you mark habits as completed
  • User Progress: XP points, level, and streak freeze counts
  • User Preferences: Display name and app settings
  • Notification Settings: Reminder times you configure for your habits

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT collect usage statistics
  • We do NOT have access to your data

How Your Data is Used

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your habits and track your progress
  • Calculate streaks, XP, and levels
  • Send local push notifications at times you specify
  • Provide a personalized experience within the app

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally using secure device storage (AsyncStorage)
  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We recommend using device-level security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID) for additional protection

Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all associated data
  • You can clear all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings

Third-Party Services

Habit Tracker does NOT integrate with any third-party services that collect data. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Push Notifications

We use Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) solely to deliver local notifications you schedule. These notifications are generated and delivered locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Habit Tracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect any information, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update any habit or setting
  • Deletion: Delete individual habits or clear all data
  • Portability: Your data exists only on your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@habittracker.app

Summary

Your privacy is guaranteed because:

  • All data stays on your device
  • We have no servers collecting your information
  • We cannot access your habits, progress, or any personal data
  • You have full control to delete your data at any time

This privacy policy is effective as of December 30, 2025.
